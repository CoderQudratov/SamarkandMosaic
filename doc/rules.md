# STRICT RULES

Never:

* flat buttons
* ugly colors
* neon colors
* generic UI
* sharp transitions
* laggy drag
* weak snap
* hard sounds
* low quality assets

Always:

* smooth drag
* exact cursor tracking
* premium gold style
* Timurid colors
* satisfying snap
* soft easing
* elegant shadows
* luxury feeling

Main Rule:

Every click must feel expensive.
